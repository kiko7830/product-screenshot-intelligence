# Example Prompts

## Quick Identification

```text
Use $product-screenshot-intelligence to identify this product from the screenshot. Give me the likely product, confidence level, evidence, and the top 3 strategic implications.
```

## Full Market Report

```text
Use $product-screenshot-intelligence to analyze this screenshot. I want product identity, market situation, customer groups, competitors, strengths, weaknesses, and why the feature shown matters.
```

## Feature Strategy

```text
Use $product-screenshot-intelligence to focus on the feature shown in this screenshot. What user problem does it solve, what metric does it likely target, what is its short-term goal, and what is its long-term strategic role?
```

## Competitive Read

```text
Use $product-screenshot-intelligence to identify this product and compare the visible feature against likely competitors. Explain where it is differentiated and where it may be table stakes.
```

## Chinese Output

```text
Use $product-screenshot-intelligence to analyze this screenshot. 用中文输出：产品识别、市场情况、客户人群、长短板，以及截图中这个功能在短期和长期目标里的水位。
```
